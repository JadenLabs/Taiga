# Taiga - Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 15 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what data **Taiga** (the "bot") collects, why, and how it is handled. By using the bot you agree to this policy. It applies only to Taiga, not to Discord itself or any other service.

## 1. What We Collect
Taiga stores the minimum needed to run the game. For each player we keep:
- Your **Discord user ID** (a numeric identifier - not your username, email, or real name).
- **Gameplay data** tied to that ID: pets, beans, golden beans, streaks and highest streak, inventory and items, generators, permanent upgrades, prestige count, achievements, lifetime beans earned, and your passive-income rate.
- **Activity timestamps** used for cooldowns and idle income: when you last pet, fished, and collected, and whether reminder messages have been sent.

We also keep a single anonymous, server-wide counter of how many commands the bot has run. It is not linked to individual users.

## 2. What We Do **Not** Collect
- ❌ Message content - the bot does not read or store your messages.
- ❌ Your real name, email address, phone number, or IP address.
- ❌ Payment information (Taiga is free and sells nothing).
- ❌ Any data from servers or channels beyond the commands you choose to run.

Your Discord display name and avatar may appear in command responses (e.g. profiles and leaderboards), but these are read live from Discord and are **not stored** by the bot.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used only to:
- Run the game (track progress, currency, cooldowns, and idle generation).
- Show profiles, stats, inventories, and leaderboards.
- Send you **direct messages** *only if* you have opted in by buying reminder items (e.g. the Alarm Clock or Streak Alarm), or for one-time achievement notifications.

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

## 4. Direct Messages
The bot may DM you for pet-cooldown reminders or streak warnings if you own the relevant items, and occasionally for achievement notices. If your DMs are closed, these simply fail and nothing happens. You can avoid all DMs by not buying the reminder items and/or closing DMs from server members.

## 5. Storage & Security
Game data is stored in a private database (MongoDB) accessible only to the bot and its operators. We take reasonable steps to protect it, but no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Sharing
We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your interactions are processed by Discord under [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). Data may be disclosed if required by law.

## 7. Data Retention & Deletion
We keep your game data while you use the bot. You may request to **view or delete** all data associated with your Discord ID at any time - see Contact below. Deleting your data erases your game progress and is irreversible.

## 8. Children's Privacy
Taiga is not directed at children under the minimum age required by Discord in their country (at least 13). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below that age.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of the bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.
